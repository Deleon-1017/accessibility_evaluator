WHAT WAS IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

THREE INTERACTIVE VIEWING MODES:

1. SIDE BY SIDE VIEW (Default)
   - Full cards showing both states simultaneously
   - Complete information with status badges and impact notes
   - Best for detailed learning

2. SLIDER COMPARE VIEW
   - Interactive slider to reveal before/after states
   - Drag with mouse, swipe on touch, arrow keys on keyboard
   - Real-time visual comparison
   - Best for spotting visual differences

3. TOGGLE VIEW
   - Switch between states with button click
   - Auto-play mode (2-second intervals)
   - Smooth transitions
   - Best for presentations and demonstrations
